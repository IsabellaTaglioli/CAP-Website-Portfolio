Growth

80s Movie Quizz

This is my project where I feel like I showed the most growth. In this website the user is able to partake in the quiz by selecting their favorite images under each category. I feel that this displays my growth the best since I took what I learned from previous works and projects to complete it. Over this course we learned how to design and customize our projects to match the topics that they were on. I struggled in the beginning to figure out how I wanted everything to be displayed in order to make it as easy as possible for the users. I went around asking friends and family for their input on how I could make this website fun for everyone. If I could change one thing about this project it would be how I matched each photo to their answers. Overall, this app helped me by challenging me to think outside of the box when it came to coding and how I want my future projects to appear.
